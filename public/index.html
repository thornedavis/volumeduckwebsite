One-minute setup

Download it, grant permission, and start talking.

  1. Download the DMG. Open it and drag Volume Duck into your Applications folder.
  2. Grant the keyboard permission macOS asks for. Depending on your macOS version, that will be Input Monitoring or Accessibility.
  3. Record your dictation hotkey. Set your duck level, tweak the fade, and you are ready.

Good to know

Volume Duck is currently tuned for your Mac's built-in output. If you swap to some external audio devices, software ducking may not be available yet.
